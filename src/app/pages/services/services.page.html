Service Offerings

Discovery and Design

Scope clarification, roadmap definition, risk mapping, and decision support.

Expected outcomes: shared goals, phased roadmap, risk visibility.

Typical duration: 2 to 6 weeks.

Main deliverables: discovery summary, scope priorities, risk log, milestone plan.

Client involvement: weekly sessions, stakeholder access, timely decisions.

Build and Delivery

Implementation, iterative releases, quality control, and delivery coordination.

Expected outcomes: regular increments, transparent progress, launch readiness.

Typical duration: 6 weeks to 9+ months.

Main deliverables: working increments, release notes, quality evidence, launch plan.

Client involvement: prioritization input, regular reviews, acceptance decisions.

Takeover and Modernization

Existing product recovery, reliability improvements, and modernization planning.

Expected outcomes: clear diagnosis, stabilized workflows, phased modernization.

Typical duration: 4 weeks to 12+ months.

Main deliverables: assessment report, stabilization plan, modernization roadmap, progress tracking.

Client involvement: system access, business priorities, modernization alignment.

Operations and Continuous Improvement

Maintenance, monitoring support, incident routines, and product evolution planning.

Expected outcomes: better visibility, faster issue resolution, predictable releases.

Typical duration: ongoing monthly engagement.

Main deliverables: operational cadence, improvement backlog, reliability reporting, quarterly recommendations.

Client involvement: recurring review meetings, shared prioritization, usage feedback.

Engagement Models

Project-Based Engagement

Best for defined initiatives with clear scope boundaries.

Pros: focused timeline, clear milestones, contained commitment.

Cons: less continuity after delivery unless extended.

Long-Term Partnership

Best for products that require ongoing evolution and operational continuity.

Pros: deep context, faster decisions, sustained reliability.

Cons: broader ongoing commitment and planning cadence.
